Our Services

Resizing/Hemming

We can resize garments that are too big or too small to ensure a perfect fit. We can shorten or lengthen the length of pants, skirts, dresses, and more.

Mending Repairs

We can repair rips, and other damage to your clothes.

Waistband & Sleeve Alterations

We can adjust the waistband of pants, skirts, and other garments to ensure a perfect fit. We can adjust the length and width of sleeves on jackets, blouses, and other garments.
